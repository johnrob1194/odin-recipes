Spaghetti

Description

This spaghetti is crafted using spaghetti, tomato sauce, some herbs, tomatoes, and some love!

Ingredients

    Spaghetti
    Tomato sauce
    Herbs
    Some Love!

Steps

    Get Ingredients
    Boil water
    Put tomato sauce and herbs in another pan & heat up
    Put spaghetti into water
    Drain water
    Enjoy!
